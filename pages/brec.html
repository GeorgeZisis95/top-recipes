Vanilla Cloud Cupcakes

Description

Fluffy, light, and soft vanilla cupcakes topped with creamy buttercream frosting. Perfect for birthdays, tea time, or any celebration!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • For frosting: 1/2 cup butter, 2 cups powdered sugar, 1 tsp vanilla, 1–2 tbsp milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  2. Cream butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Stir in vanilla.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk flour, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Gradually add dry ingredients to wet mixture, alternating with milk, starting and ending with flour.
  6. Divide batter evenly into cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full.
  7. Bake for 18–22 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean. Cool completely.
  8. Prepare frosting by beating butter, powdered sugar, vanilla, and milk until smooth and fluffy. Frost cupcakes once cooled.

Nutritional Info (per cupcake)

  • Calories: 220 kcal
  • Protein: 3 g
  • Fat: 10 g
  • Carbohydrates: 32 g
  • Fiber: 0.5 g
  • Sugar: 22 g
