Peachy Dream Smoothie

Description

A creamy, refreshing peach smoothie with a hint of vanilla, perfect for a sunny morning or a mid-day pick-me-up. Sweet, smooth, and naturally vibrant!

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe peaches, peeled and sliced
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Ice cubes (optional, for a chilled smoothie)

Instructions

  1. Place the peaches, Greek yogurt, orange juice, honey, and vanilla extract into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy. Add ice cubes if you want a colder, thicker smoothie.
  3. Taste and adjust sweetness if necessary by adding a little more honey.
  4. Pour into a glass and serve immediately. Garnish with a peach slice if desired.

Nutritional Info (per serving)

  • Calories: 180 kcal
  • Protein: 6 g
  • Fat: 2 g
  • Carbohydrates: 35 g
  • Fiber: 3 g
  • Sugar: 28 g
